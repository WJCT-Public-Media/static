6 Concerts to See this Week in Jacksonville

Omni at the Jessie, Rock the Dock Festival, Vitamin String Quartet and more

By JME Staff

Our weekly Go concert recommendations are updated every week. For a comprehensive list of this week’s concerts, go to our live music calendar page.

Country

Danielle Todd & Grady James – Thursday, January 30

Blue Jay Listening Room | Jacksonville Beach

Two Nashville-based country artists, Danielle Todd and Grady James, perform at the Blue Jay Listening Room on Thursday.

Classical/Pop

Vitamin String Quartet – Thursday, January 30

Florida Theatre | Downtown Jacksonville

Los Angeles-based ensemble Vitamin String Quartet performs classical interpretations of pop hits at the Florida Theatre on Thursday.

Punk/Pop Punk

Rock the Dock – Saturday, February 1

Grace Note Brewing | Lakeshore

Local pop-punk heroes Inspection 12 headline the Rock the Dock Music and Beer Festival at Grace Note Brewing, with support from Idle Acres, Ticker Tapeworm Parade, Friendly Fire, Tides and more on Saturday.

Post Punk

Omni – Saturday, February 1

The Jessie | Downtown Jacksonville

Atlanta post punks Omni headline the first installment of February’s Winterland Presents: Our Music Our Future series on the loading docks of the Jessie Ball duPont center on Saturday, with support from Canadian dance-punks La Sécurité and local shoegaze/psych band Ducats.

Jazz

Adam Hawley – Saturday, February 1

The Ritz | Downtown Jacksonville

Portland, OR-based contemporary jazz guitarist Adam Hawley performs at the Ritz Theatre on Saturday.

Hip Hop

Black Drum – Saturday, February 1

Underbelly | Downtown Jacksonville

Black Drum reunites at Underbelly with support from local band Universal Green, Duval emcee/producer Willie Evans Jr. and singer-songwriter 1BlackFrend on Saturday.
